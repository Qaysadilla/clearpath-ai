ClearPath AI is an AI document-to-action assistant for newcomers, international students, first-generation students, and people who struggle with confusing documents, emails, forms, and official instructions.

Problem:
Important documents are often confusing. People may not understand what the document means, what deadline matters, what documents they need, what action they must take, or how to respond. Missing these steps can cause stress, delays, missed appointments, or lost opportunities.

Solution:
A user pastes document text into the app. ClearPath AI turns it into:
1. Plain-language summary
2. Important deadlines
3. Required actions

5. Risk level: low, medium, high
6. Step-by-step checklist
7. Draft reply email
8. Optional simpler explanation for ESL or low-literacy users

Important boundary:
ClearPath AI does not provide legal, immigration, financial, or medical advice. It only helps users understand documents, organize next steps, and prepare communication.
